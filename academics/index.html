Academics

Students should know there are special moments that pull everything we have learned into focus. When theory, practice, experience, and talent all come to one sharp point—a decision that shapes a definitive course of action. When it's no longer an issue of what can be done, but of what you will do.

Engineer Program

Henerton School Engineer Class is a unique 3 years program.
This 3 years curriculum provides students with:

  • A multidisciplinary scientific training that gives students a broad scientific base (basic sciences, engineering sciences, social economics).
  • A human and sports training that allows students to develop their behavioral and relational skills.
  • Specialized courses with advanced scientific concentrations that rival the top engineering schools worldwide.
  • Courses in both English and French

General studies are offered during the first two years of the program, where students acquire a solid scientific foundation in six subjects out of the eight offered by the Academic Departments: Pure and Applied Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mechanics, Economics and Computer Science.

This multidisciplinary education is in large part what gives Henerton School graduates their high potential for evolution in their careers. It prepares them to implent soft skills during their careers. Il also prepares them to practice different managing professions. Thus students are well-suited to evolve into high-level positions in management and coordination.
